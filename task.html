Making Parota and Alu Curry

1.Take parota powder and mix it with water properly

2.Roll out the dough and cook on hot griddle with oil

3.For alu curry,onions,Tomato,and spices in pan

4.Add boiled potatoes and cook until the curry thickness

Biryani Making

1.Take meat and cook 50%

2.cook rice until 70%

3.Layer the marinated meat and rice in pot

4.cook on low heat until fully cooked
